Our Story

From a Small Repair Shop to a Full-Service Tech Company

Konjyosom Tech Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to make quality technology services accessible to everyone in our community. What started as a small computer repair shop at Konjyosom 4 has grown into a comprehensive technology solutions provider.

Today, we serve over 500 clients across Nepal, from individual homeowners to large enterprises. Our team of 50+ certified technicians and engineers brings expertise in hardware repair, network infrastructure, cloud computing, access-control, and custom software development.

We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients through transparent pricing, quality workmanship, and exceptional customer service.
